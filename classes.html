Our Music Classes

Musical journeys designed for every stage of early development, from newborn to three years old.

0 – 12 Months

Baby Classes

Our Baby Classes are a gentle introduction to the world of music. We focus on sensory play, bonding, and early brain development through soft sounds, lullabies, and rhythmic movement.

Duration

45 Minutes per session

Activities

Singing, Movement, Gentle Sensory Play

Learning Outcomes
  • Sensory Recognition
  • Parental Bonding
  • Emotional Regulation

1 – 3 Years

Toddler Classes

Designed for the energetic and curious toddler, these classes encourage active participation through percussion instruments, dancing, and musical games that promote physical and social skills.

Duration

45 Minutes per session

Activities

Drumming, Dancing, Group Singing

Learning Outcomes
  • Rhythm & Coordination
  • Social Interaction Skills
  • Language Development

All Ages (0 – 5 Years)

Mixed Age Classes

Perfect for families with more than one child, our Mixed Age classes create a collaborative environment where siblings can learn from each other and participate in musical play together.

Duration

45 Minutes per session

Activities

Storytelling, Collaborative Play, Multi-Instrument

Learning Outcomes
  • Family Bonding
  • Peer-to-Peer Learning
  • Creative Expression

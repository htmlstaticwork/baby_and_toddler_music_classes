The Joyful Pedagogy

Play-First Learning

We believe music is caught, not taught. Every lesson is structured as as adventurous play session.

Rhythm-Rooted

Steady beat is the foundation of all later learning, from math to coordination and social movement.

Sensory Milestones

Using tactile instruments and visual cues to reach cognitive developmental milestones with joy.

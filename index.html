Why Music for Kids?

Brain Development

Music stimulates all areas of child development and skills for school readiness, including intellectual, social-emotional, and language literacy.

Bonding & Joy

Creating music together creates deep emotional bonds between parent and child in a safe, joyful environment.

Fun Learning

We believe children learn best when they are having fun and are actively engaged in play.
